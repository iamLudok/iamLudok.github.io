Privacy & Data

Our data is one of our most valuable assets — yet we hand it over to corporations without a second thought. I believe in self-hosting, owning your infrastructure, and being conscious of what you store and how. Depending on others for your own data is a vulnerability, not a convenience.

AI Responsibility

AI is one of the most powerful tools we have right now — and that's exactly why we need to understand what we're building. Shipping code you don't understand to a client isn't a shortcut, it's a risk. If everyone builds things they can't explain, we're heading somewhere bad. Read the code. Know what it does.
